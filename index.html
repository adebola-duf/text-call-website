Features

App Themes

2 app themes which suits your needs:
Light & Dark. I prefer dark tho

Easy Navigation

Enjoy a user-friendly interface with a clean design that makes navigating the app effortless.

Simple and Custom Messages

Send a simple message with just text and a customizable background for your text, or create a custom message with audio, video, rich text, and images.

Urgent Messaging

Ensure your message is seen with the urgency of a phone call. TextCall sends messages that ring out like a call.

Fun and Engaging

Trust me, it is fun just sending text calls back and forth to your friends! Enjoy a new way of communicating that is both urgent and entertaining.
